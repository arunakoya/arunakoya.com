Meet Franz

I like this messenger app, very much. It supports so many different messaging platforms, that it's actually become more valuable to me, than any single messaging platform. It is also free, but you have to wait 10 seconds, before messaging, every so often. The paid version, isn't that expensive though. Available on Windows, Mac and Linux.
