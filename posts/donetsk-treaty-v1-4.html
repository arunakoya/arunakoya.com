Donetsk Treaty v1.4

Memorandum of Understanding.

Russia confirms it will not incur on any external territory(land, air, sea) and adopt a defensive posture only.

Russia confirms the borders with Ukraine, remain as before the conflict started with the DMZ in Donetsk in place.

Leases in Ukraine are available to be purchased as open trading commences 15th June 2026.

15th June 2026 - End of Ukraine / Russia conflict. 

15th June 2026 UK have lifted all sanctions on Russia.

15th June 2026 European Parliament have decided to lift  sanctions.

Any incurrences will bring back sanctions from all parties for 3years.

Agreed in the presence of Queen Arun Sandhya.
